Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access the sample papers? +

You can access the sample papers by selecting the appropriate subject tab from the main page. Once you've selected a subject, all available papers for that subject will be displayed. Click on the "View" button to open the paper.

Are these papers officially provided by educational institutions? +

Our collection includes both official papers provided by educational institutions and high-quality practice papers created by educators. Each paper is clearly labeled with its source.

How often is new content added? +

We add new content at the beginning of each academic term. Major updates occur in September and January, with smaller updates throughout the year as new materials become available.

Is there a mobile app available? +

Currently, we don't have a dedicated mobile app, but our website is fully responsive and works well on all mobile devices. You can add it to your home screen for quick access.

Can I contribute my own sample papers? +

Yes! We welcome contributions from educators. Please use the contact form below to get in touch with us about contributing materials. All submissions will be reviewed for quality and relevance before being added.
